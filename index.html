What I can do for you

I am a web developer, designer, and educator from the Philippines. I specialize in creating front ends for web projects and integrating them with APIs and back end processes. I also design web experiences with particular attention to making them accessible and robust. The projects I work on are not just responsive but also light and resilient even on older devices.

Here are some of the things I can do for you:

  • Create reusable responsive layouts using CSS
  • Use the newest technology on the web but also make your site still work on older browsers
  • Create lightweight front end interfaces for your back end service or API
  • Translate your designs into actual working code
  • Design a site for your business needs.
  • Make your site accessible and compliant to the WCAG.

If this sounds good to you, you can email me at devFrancisRubio@gmail.com. We can talk and arrange for things if you want to hire me as a freelance developer or a member of your team.
